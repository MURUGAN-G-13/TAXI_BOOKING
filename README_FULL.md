### Quick Start

1. **Open the Application**
   ```bash
   Open index.html in your web browser
   ```

2. **Navigate the Website**
   - Use the top navigation menu to explore sections
   - Home, The Car, Service, Payment, and Book Now buttons

3. **Make a Booking**
   - Click "Book Now" button
   - Enter your pickup and drop-off locations
   - Select a vehicle type
   - Review the fare estimate
   - Complete your payment

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### Payment & Booking

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept credit cards, debit cards, digital wallets (Google Pay, Apple Pay), and cash payments.

**Q: Can I schedule a ride in advance?**
A: Yes, you can book rides up to 7 days in advance through our booking system.

**Q: What if I need to cancel my booking?**
A: You can cancel anytime. Cancellation charges apply based on timing:
- Free cancellation within 2 minutes
- 50% charge if cancelled within 2-5 minutes
- Full charge if cancelled after 5 minutes

### Safety & Verification

**Q: Are your drivers background-checked?**
A: Yes, all drivers undergo:
- Police verification
- Medical fitness check
- Driving license validation
- Customer reference checks

**Q: How is my safety ensured?**
A: We have multiple safety measures:
- Real-time ride tracking
- Driver identification verification
- Emergency contact button in app
- 24/7 support team monitoring

### Pricing & Corporate

**Q: How is the fare calculated?**
A: Fares are based on:
- Base fare for vehicle type
- Distance traveled
- Time taken (only if idle)
- Surge pricing during peak hours

**Q: Do you offer corporate accounts?**
A: Yes! We provide:
- Special corporate rates
- Dedicated account manager
- Monthly invoicing
- Expense reports
- Bulk booking discounts
